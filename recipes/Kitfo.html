Another dish that’s often served with Ethiopia’s star flatbread, Injera, is Kitfo. This is a dish beloved for its rich flavors as well as its unique preparation. What’s in it? It’s made of raw, minced beef seasoned with a spicy chili powder blend called mitmita, plus clarified butter infused with herbs and spices. Kitfo is traditionally eaten raw, which might be surprising for many Westerners. However, eating raw meat dishes like Kitfo is a celebrated part of Ethiopian cuisine.
