beyaynet is popular during religious fasting periods, beyaynetu is a vegetarian platter that features various vegetable dishes all served together. The dishes most commonly included are a red lentil stew called misir wot, a split-pea stew called kik wat, gomen (collard greens or kale), dinich ena karat alicha (boiled potato with cabbage), shiro, and key sire alicha (potato, beet, and carrot stew)
